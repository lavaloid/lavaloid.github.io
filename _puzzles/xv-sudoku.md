## Rules

-   **Normal sudoku rules apply.**  
    Every row, column, and 3 &times; 3 box contains all digits 1-9.
-   **XV rules apply (no negatives).**  
    Cells separated by an X or V must sum to 10 or 5, respectively. Not all XV's are given.
