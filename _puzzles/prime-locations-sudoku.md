## Rules

-   **Normal sudoku rules apply.**  
    Every row, column, and 3 &times; 3 box contains all digits 1-9.
-   **Prime pairs rule.**  
    Along the line, any pair of neighboring digits sum to a prime number.
-   Cells marked with a circle are prime numbers.
